Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Today

1. Information We Collect

When you use EventQR, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your username, email address, and hashed password when you sign up.
  • Event Data: Attendee names, optional phone numbers, table numbers, and seat numbers used to generate QR codes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to provide the core functionality of the application: managing seating arrangements, verifying attendees, and allowing you to access your personal booking history.

3. Data Retention & Automatic Deletion

We respect your privacy and do not store event data indefinitely. All QR codes, attendee details, and bulk booking data are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers 90 days after generation.

4. Security

We take security seriously. Your passwords are securely hashed using industry-standard cryptography (PBKDF2 SHA-256), and your active sessions are managed securely. However, no internet-based service is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact Page.
